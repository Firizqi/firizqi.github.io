About Me

The Godfather

The Corleone crime family is one of the Five Families operating in New York and in other parts of the United States. The family was founded by Vito Corleone, who fronted his operations by starting the Genco Olive Oil Company. They became the most powerful crime family in New York after defeating Giuseppe Mariposa, who was once the boss of all bosses, during the Olive Oil War in the early 1930s.

Name : Vito Corleone (Andolini)
Born : December 7, 1891 - July 29, 1955
Title : Don, The Godfather

Vito Corleone was the founder and Don of the Corleone family. Vito was known to be very loyal and generous to his friends and allies, but would always get vengeance if betrayed. He was also a very loving and caring father and husband to his family.

Name : Santino "Sonny" Corleone, Sr.
Born : 1916 - 1948
Title : Caporegime, Underboss, Acting Don

Santino "Sonny" Corleone, Sr. was the eldest son of the Vito Corleone and the acting Don of the Corleone family. Though he was fiercely loyal to his family, he was known for his violent temper and rash decisions.

Name : Thomas "Tom" Hagen
Born : 1916 - 1970s
Title : Consigliere, Acting Don

Thomas "Tom" Hagen was the consigliere and head lawyer of the Corleone family, as well as being the adoptive son of Vito Corleone. Alongside Michael, he is the only child of Vito Corleone to have gone to college.

Name : Frederico "Fredo" Corleone
Born : 1919 - 1959
Title : Underboss

Frederico "Fredo" Corleone was the second oldest of the Corleone brothers. As an adult he was seen as the weakest of the three brothers and was given relatively unimportant business to run.

Name : Michael Corleone
Born : March 23, 1920 - 1997
Title : Don, The Godfather

Michael Corleone was the Don of the Corleone family after his father Vito Corleone stepped down. When Vito dies in 1955, and Michael officially becomes Don.
